## Project Deep-Dive: Markov-Based Predictive Maintenance (5 minutes)

### The Business Problem
*"Industrial equipment failures cost manufacturers millions in unplanned downtime. Traditional maintenance is either reactive (too late) or calendar-based (inefficient). The opportunity was to create an intelligent system that predicts failures 37-49 hours in advance and automatically orchestrates the response."*

### Technical Innovation
*"I developed a Markov Chain model that achieved 73.5% directional accuracy in predicting equipment health states. But the real innovation was architecting the complete automation layer around it:"*

**Core Components:**
1. **Edge Computing**: AWS Greengrass deployment for real-time processing
2. **Intelligent Analysis**: AI that explains why equipment is failing, not just that it's failing
3. **Workflow Automation**: n8n orchestration that automatically routes alerts based on criticality
4. **Multi-Channel Response**: Smart notifications via Slack, email, SMS based on urgency and role

### Business Results
*"This delivered $8.4M annual savings with a 1-month payback period—but more importantly, it eliminated reactive maintenance culture and enabled proactive operational excellence."*
